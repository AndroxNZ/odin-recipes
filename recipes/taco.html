Easy Taco Skillet

Description

Easy taco skillet bake that uses only one pan. This recipe can be made for under $10!

Ingredients/h3>
  1. 1 pound ground beef
  2. 1 onion, chopped
  3. 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  4. 2 cups water
  5. 1 cup converted rice
  6. 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning
  7. 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  8. 1 cup shredded lettuce
