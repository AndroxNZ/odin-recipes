Quick and Easy Honey-Garlic Pork Chops

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for pork chops in a flavorful honey-garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  1. 4 (5 ounce) pork chops
  2. salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  3. 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  4. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  5. 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  6. 6 cloves garlic, minced
  7. ¼ cup honey¼ cup honey
  8. ¼ cup water
  9. 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
